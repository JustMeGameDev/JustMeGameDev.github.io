Big Cheese Donte

A little about the game



This is a RPG prototype game based around a 1920 chicago maffia setting. You play as the son of a low ranking maffia boss. He has asked you to proof your capable of taking over his empire.

therefore you need to collect grunts and battle other crime bosses in the city to take over their territory. The ultimate goal is to take over the biggest rival of your dad's empire (Big Faluci).

when you won that battle you get the name Big Cheese Donte and you are the new kingpin of the city!

things i'm proud of!
